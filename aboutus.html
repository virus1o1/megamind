Our Story



Mega was originally opened as Mega Seafood in 2005, housed in a historic space on Broad Street.
Four of our owners made it this far with nothing but scratch, and driven by their passion for incredible food and the hospitality industry.

The restaurant gave Siam a home to work from while still focusing on seafood, a culinary corner he has long excelled in, while also establishing his local restaurants– Mega Bistro and Main Street Meats– as pillars in the Chattanooga food scene.
